# Code of Conduct

HALO is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for
everyone involved in the project.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language in Korean and English.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the end users (elderly
  and vulnerable scam-target populations).

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment of any kind.
- Discriminatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Violations should be reported to dayeon603@gmail.com. All reports will be
reviewed confidentially. Consequences may include warnings, temporary
bans, or permanent bans from project spaces.

This code is adapted from the Contributor Covenant version 2.1.
